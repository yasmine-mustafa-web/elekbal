Welcome To Our School


We Believe Nothing Is More Important Than Education

El Ekbal Language School – Girls Department, located in Abis under the Montazah Educational Zone in Alexandria, is one of the distinguished branches of the El Ekbal Schools Complex. Founded in 1978 by Mr. Nabih Zahran,the school has built a strong reputation for providing high-quality education that blends academic excellence with advanced language learning. The Girls Department continues this legacy by fostering students’ knowledge, character, and confidence within a respectful, motivating, and inspiring learning environment. Through dedicated teachers, supportive values, and a commitment to continuous development,El Ekbal ensures that every student is prepared for a bright and successful future.
